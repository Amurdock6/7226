ALL DONATIONS.........go directly to programs for children.
There are no paid employees with Kids Read.
Our Volunteers are qualified professionals and we love what we do.

With your help we have provided masks,
sleeping nets and hand washing stations to our children in 5 communities.
The first KidsRead "Little Library" premiered on August 4,
2019 with the sole purpose of providing books to the under-served children in
Uganda. They are encouraged to take a book, read it and return it for another book.
There are 150 books or more in each library.
They range from picture books for the very young, story books, some early tutorials
and work books.
We purchase books from local publishers and use local artisans to build and paint
our libraries.
These will return after the end of the pandemic.
